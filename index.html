OnaTrack is compatible with all motorcycles and can be mounted securely on any bike. The device uses GPS and internal sensors, so it doesn't need to connect to your motorcycle's electronics.

The battery lasts over 8 hours of continuous use, which covers a full day at the track. It charges via USB-C and takes approximately 2 hours for a full charge. A battery indicator in the app keeps you informed.
